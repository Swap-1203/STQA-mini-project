The Benefits of Eating Organic

Organic foods are grown without the use of synthetic pesticides, fertilizers, or genetically modified organisms (GMOs). This means they are healthier for you and better for the environment. Studies have shown that organic foods often contain more beneficial nutrients, such as antioxidants, than their conventionally-grown counterparts.
